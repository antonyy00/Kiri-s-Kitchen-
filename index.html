Kiri's Kitchen

Information

Established in 2015, Kiri’s Kitchen is a cozy, family-owned restaurant located in the heart of San Francisco. We serve a fusion of traditional and modern American cuisine, focusing on fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Our warm, welcoming atmosphere is designed to make every diner feel at home while enjoying hearty, delicious meals.
